After you have moved on from learning the hidden probabilities phase 2 begins. In phase 2 your task is to reach or exceed a point threshold by accumulating points from the options.

In phase 2 the options are the same as in phase 1 (i.e. the hidden probabilities and options will be the same as in phase 1). However your task is not to learn about hidden probabilities but to accumulate points.

You start with 0 points. A progress bar shows your points and the threshold you want to reach or exceed (e.g., 5 points and a threshold of 29, shown below).

Clicking on an option causes the option to play out a point draw according to the hidden probabilities. This increases your points and your points will be shown in a progress bar (shown below). Your task is to reach or exceed the threshold in 5 clicks or choices.
