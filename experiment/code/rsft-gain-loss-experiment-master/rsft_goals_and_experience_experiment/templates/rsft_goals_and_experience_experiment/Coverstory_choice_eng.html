After you have moved on from learning the hidden probabilities phase 2 begins. The hidden probabilities and options will be the same as in phase 1.

Similar to phase 1 clicking causes the option to play out a point draw according to the probabilities and show the drawn amount of points. In phase 2 however the point amount will be accumulated – meaning every click matters. You must click or choose 5 times.

The accumulated points will be shown in a progress bar (shown below). In this progress bar you will see an additional value – this is your threshold in this phase. Your task in phase 2 is to reach or exceed the threshold in 5 clicks or choices.
